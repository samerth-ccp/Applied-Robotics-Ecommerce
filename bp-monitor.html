Questions & Answers

Q: How accurate is this compared to hospital monitors?

A: This monitor has clinical-grade accuracy of ±3mmHg, which is equivalent to professional medical equipment used in hospitals and clinics.

Q: Can it detect irregular heartbeats?

A: Yes, the monitor has built-in arrhythmia detection and will alert you if irregular heartbeat patterns are detected during measurement.

Q: What cuff size should I choose?

A: Measure your upper arm circumference. Small: 22-32cm, Medium: 32-42cm, Large: 42-48cm. Proper cuff size is essential for accurate readings.
