Product Description

The Smart Ring by Applied Robotics represents the pinnacle of wearable health technology. This revolutionary device transforms your finger into a comprehensive health monitoring station, providing continuous, medical-grade tracking of your vital signs without compromising on style or comfort.

Crafted from premium titanium with a bio-compatible coating, the Smart Ring is designed for 24/7 wear. Its advanced sensor array includes photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors for heart rate monitoring, temperature sensors for body temperature tracking, and motion sensors for activity detection and sleep analysis.

Whether you're an athlete looking to optimize performance, a health-conscious individual monitoring wellness trends, or someone with specific health concerns requiring continuous monitoring, the Smart Ring provides the insights you need to make informed decisions about your health.

Questions & Answers

Q: How accurate is the heart rate monitoring?

A: The Smart Ring uses medical-grade PPG sensors with clinical accuracy of ±2 BPM, validated against ECG monitoring in independent studies.

Q: Can I wear it while swimming?

A: Yes! The Smart Ring has IP68 water resistance rating and can be worn while swimming, showering, or in any water activity up to 100 meters depth.

Q: How do I know what size to order?

A: We recommend ordering our free sizing kit first, or you can measure your finger circumference with a string and compare to our size chart. The ring should fit snugly but comfortably.
