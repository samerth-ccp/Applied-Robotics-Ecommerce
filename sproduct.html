Smart Ring - Advanced Health Monitor

$249.99

Product Details

The Smart Ring by Applied Robotics is a revolutionary health monitoring device that tracks your vital signs 24/7. This state-of-the-art wearable technology features continuous heart rate monitoring, sleep tracking, activity detection, and temperature sensing. With its sleek titanium design and advanced sensors, the Smart Ring provides medical-grade accuracy while maintaining comfort for all-day wear. The device syncs seamlessly with your smartphone and offers up to 7 days of battery life on a single charge.

Key Features

  • 24/7 Heart Rate Monitoring
  • Sleep Stage Tracking
  • Activity & Workout Detection
  • Body Temperature Monitoring
  • 7-Day Battery Life
  • Waterproof Design (IP68)
  • Smartphone App Integration
  • Medical-Grade Accuracy
