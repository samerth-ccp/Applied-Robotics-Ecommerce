Product Details

The Applied Robotics Smart Ring is a cutting-edge wearable health monitor that delivers comprehensive biometric tracking in a sleek, comfortable design. This medical-grade device continuously monitors your heart rate, tracks sleep patterns, measures activity levels, and provides detailed health insights through our advanced mobile application. Built with titanium construction and sapphire crystal sensors, the ring offers exceptional durability while maintaining a lightweight feel. The device features advanced optical sensors for precise heart rate variability measurement, temperature sensing for fever detection, and motion sensors for activity tracking. Perfect for healthcare professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone committed to optimizing their health and wellness journey.
